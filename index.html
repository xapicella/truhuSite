About Me

I'm Xander Apicella, a fourth year student at UCSB pursuing a Physics BS Major and a Professional Writing Minor via the Science Communication Track. Outside my studies, I also work as the Project Manager for UCSB's IEEE Student Branch, Science & Tech Editor for The Bottom Line Newspaper, Co-President of UCSB's MSO Student Branch, and a systems engineering contractor for Lockheed Martin.

My principle interests are in engineering exciting and useful projects, and using those experiences to learn and communicate those learned lessons effectively. Though much of my academic effort has been focused on physics and engineering, I am a life-long writing addict and plan on honing storytelling and scientific communication skills wherever possible in the future. In my free time I like to go rock climbing, get coffee with friends, and write/read fiction.
