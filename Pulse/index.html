TruHu Pulse

Create a Connected, Productive Workforce

My HR Portal

A simple, elegant and customizable way to share all HR and enterprise information in an easily accessible and centralized location. No more emailing employees documents or putting them on an intranet nobody uses! Provide fingertip access to documents like handbooks, benefits or policies or links to 401k, open enrollment and learning management systems. My HR Portal can be customized with your company’s branding and can be configured to deliver personalized information (think benefits) based on employees’ profiles.

Pulse Surveys

Now more than ever, companies need to monitor and manage employee well-being. Enhance your ability to capture and report employee engagement and satisfaction so your organization can identify where to focus their efforts to improve company culture and employee performance. Leverage TruHu’s library of almost 200 HR approved questions on topics like Productivity, Diversity, Performance, Satisfaction, Benefits, COVID-19, and more. Get clear reporting to view results, explore trends and share with management and employees.

Dynamic Messaging

Always be sure important messages reach an increasingly remote and mobile workforce. With our trackable text and emails, HR and management will always know the open rate of every text or email sent. Easily resend to employees who did not view the message to ensure all critical alerts get read. Send links, images, attachments or HTML with confidence the message will always be delivered. Personalize and segment communications to individuals, departments, locations or the entire company. TruHu Pulse sends messages in multiple languages, allowing effective communications to a diverse workforce.

My Benefits Portal

A tool developed for brokers to help ensure your employees get the most out of their benefits. My Benefits Portal, which works with any benefits administration system, provides employees with one-click access to benefits information, including open enrollment docs, forms and links to transactional systems. Launch annual benefits surveys—by leveraging TruHu question database and/or creating your own—and receive clear, compelling reporting about what is most important to employees. With HR’s approval, brokers can send messages to employees, including open enrollment, benefit notifications or health-related (e.g. Breast Cancer Awareness Month, Flu Shots, etc.).
