My HR Portal

A simple, elegant and customizable way to centralize all important HR information in a place that employees will actually use. Design your portal in the way that works best for you and your organization. Deliver personalized benefit information based on employee profile data.

Pulse Surveys

Now more than ever, companies need to monitor and manage employee well-being. Enhance your ability to collect and report employee sentiment, satisfaction and engagement. Leverage TruHu’s library of almost 200 HR approved questions on topics like COVID-19, Productivity, Diversity, Performance, Satisfaction, Benefits and more. Get clear reporting to view results, explore trends and share with management and employees.

Dynamic Messaging

Always be sure important messages and critical alerts reach an increasingly remote and mobile workforce with trackable text and emails. Know the open rate of every text or email sent. Send message to an individual, department(s), location(s) or the entire company. Send multi-lingual messages that use Latin-based characters (e.g. Spanish).

My Benefits Portal

A tool developed for your broker to help ensure employees get the most out of their benefits. Portal works with any benefits admin system. Provide employees one-click access to benefits information, including open enrollment docs, forms and links to transactional systems. Launch annual benefits surveys (leverage TruHu questions or create your own) and receive clear, compelling reporting about what your employees need. Brokers can create messages that can be sent to employees (via HR admin), including open enrollment, benefit notifications or health-related (e.g. Breast Cancer Awareness Month, Flu Shots, etc.).
