A Look Into UCSB’s Kavli Institute for Theoretical Physics

This article describes the founding of one of UCSB's most important institutions, the Kavli Institute for Theoretical Physics, and discusses some of its past, present, and future impacts. I enjoyed interviewing and writing about this story immensely, and it is the small details I found in my conversations with my interviewees—humble despite the colossal shadows they cast in the myriad branches of physics—that painted a rich canvas of struggle, triumph, and that triumph’s gifts to the world.
