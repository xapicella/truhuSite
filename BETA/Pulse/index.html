Pulse

TruHu's application is a comprehensive online tool that keeps your employees passionate and up to speed

Our app is packed with functionality to get your company running smoothly with your HR admin's hands on the reins. TruHu allows your employees to track their employment and onboarding materials, all the while providing easy access to company notifications, courses, and surveys.

Your HR admins will be able to keep track of data on employee engagement, course completion, and profile information all in one place. These tools offer fine control over the entire company experience—fostering positive culture and a high employee retention rate.

My HR Portal

My HR Portal is the resource center for all employees using TruHu. In it they can view their important documents, trainings, benefits, and more. All these modules and more can be added or removed quickly from the employee interface.

Pulse Surveys

Pulse Surveys provide employer with means to gauge employee engagement, enthusiasm, and company culture. Make your own questions or leverage TruHu's HR-approved examples to give your employees more agency and motivation.

Dynamic Messaging & More

Create texts or emails that track views with links to PDFs, videos, and websites. Send them to specifics departments, locations, and groups and easily resend to those who haven't opened them.
