Our Story

Throughout my career I’ve felt intimately aware of and connected to the problems inherent to HR, especially those of small and medium businesses. I innately understood the Human in HR; I built products and services for this underserved market that leveraged the human touch.

Yet there I was, CEO of a successful medium-sized business called ThinkHR, serving hundreds of thousands of Human Resource professionals across the USA, facing a human problem.

My best employees were leaving...to work for larger companies. Not for higher pay but for a better employee experience. I was blown away.

The more I explored the issue, the more intrigued I became. At its core, employee experience means the difference between success and failure. Countless statistics made it clear that engaged, satisfied workers at large companies such as Microsoft, Facebook, and Google were dramatically more productive, had significantly less turnover, and made their employer more profitable. They had figured out how to deliver the human experience at scale.

The inspiration for my next company was born.

The time has come to level the playing field and provide the same tools and opportunities to the small- to medium-sized companies that larger companies received long ago.

That’s why I've joined forces with Cathy Reilly, an accomplished career HR executive. Together we founded Truly Human Inc. (TruHu), an HR tech company for the little guys.

Pete Yozzo, CEO

Our Mission

To make an amazing employee experience possible at every aspiring company, regardless of size or industry.

TruHu (Truly Human, Inc.) was started in 2019 with a simple but powerful goal: help make an amazing employee experience possible at every company, regardless of size or industry. We at TruHu know that companies that deliver an exceptional employee experience enjoy greater productivity, reduced turnover and a stronger culture.

Developed by former benefits brokers and successful HR Tech entrepreneurs—including the founder of both Blaise Software (now Ebix) and ThinkHR— TruHu provides Brokers and Employers with simple tools that help them onboard, manage, and analyze their workforce.

TruHu's first product is Pulse, which provides employers with a mobile-first way to keep employees connected, engaged, and productive, no matter where they are.
