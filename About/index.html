Our Story

Throughout my career I’ve always felt keenly aware of and connected to the issues HR faced, especially those at small-and medium businesses. I innately understood the “human” in HR, so much so that I built products and services for this underserved market that specifically leveraged the human touch.

Yet there I was, now CEO of a successful medium-sized business called ThinkHR, serving hundreds of thousands of Human Resource professionals across the USA, with a “human” HR problem.

My best employees are leaving to work for other large companies not because of pay but because of a better employee experience. I was blown away.

The more I explored this issue, the more intrigued I became. At its core, a good or bad employee experience meant the difference between success and failure. Countless statistics I reviewed made it clear that engaged, satisfied employees at large companies like [put companies here] were dramatically more productive, had significantly less turnover, and their employer was markedly more profitable. It was clear they had figured how to deliver the “human” experience at scale.

And the inspiration for my next company was born.

The time has come to level the playing field and provide the same analysis, tools and opportunities to the small- to medium-sized companies that larger companies received long ago. That is why I was happy to connect with Cathy Reilly, an accomplished career HR executive with the same DNA, to found Truly Human, Inc (TruHu).

Our mission: Make an amazing employee experience possible at every aspiring company, regardless of size or industry.
