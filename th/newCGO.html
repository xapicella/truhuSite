Truly Human Inc. (TruHu) Announces New Chief Growth Officer - Former Mineral Executive Joy Justus joins TruHu to drive the company’s growth strategies and strengthen TruHu’s market leadership

October 6, 2021 | Pleasanton, Ca. – Truly Human Inc. (TruHu), a leader in employee experience and communication solutions, announced the appointment of Joy Justus to the newly created role of Chief Growth Officer (CGO). Joy will play a pivotal role in accelerating the next phase of the company’s growth by expanding strategic partnerships and customers nationally.

Joy brings more than 20 years of senior leadership experience in the technology, human resources, and insurance industries. Prior to joining TruHu, she served as Senior Vice President at Mineral where she held executive leadership positions in sales, marketing, and customer experience. Joy has a successful track record of driving innovation and rapid growth at companies who distribute solutions via the insurance broker and payroll channels. She previously held key roles at OneDigital and EyeMed Vision Care when both companies were early stage.

“Joy is an accomplished leader with experience driving sales and moving organizations forward,” said Pete Yozzo, CEO and Founder of TruHu. “She was instrumental at Mineral where she joined as a startup, and it was under her leadership the company accelerated rapid growth and emerged as a market leader. We are delighted to have Joy on our executive leadership team as TruHu boldly grows its market reach nationally.”

"It’s more challenging than ever for small and medium-sized businesses to retain talent and build a successful organization,” said Justus. “Employee engagement is key and a significant opportunity exists for brokers and payroll companies to differentiate their services by offering TruHu’s innovative employee experience and communication solutions to address these challenges. I’m thrilled to join Pete again and bring our fresh new solutions to market.”
