TruHu Pulse

Create a Connected, Productive Workforce

A new mobile engagement solution, provides companies with a flexible, easy-to-administer communication and information platform. You can quickly text your employees’ important updates, engage them in surveys and post benefits information in an easy-to-use format. Since this is a web-based, employees can access this information from anywhere.

Dynamic Messaging

Ensure important communications reach an increasingly mobile workforce. With our trackable texts and emails, know a message’s open rate, and easily resend to those who did not view it. Customize your messages with links, images, attachments, HTML, and multilingual options. Segment them by individuals, departments, or office locations.

Surveys

Now more than ever, companies need to monitor employee well-being. Enhance your organization’s ability to report employee engagement, improve company culture, and boost employee performance. Leverage TruHu’s library of almost 200 HR approved questions on Productivity, Diversity, Satisfaction, Benefits, COVID-19 and more.

Portal

A simple, customizable way to centralize and share important HR, benefits, and enterprise information. Provide employees quick access to materials like handbooks, benefits/company policies, 401k links, open enrollment, and learning management systems. Customize your Portal with your company’s branding and use it to deliver personalized employee information.
