EPIC Insurance Brokers Partner with TruHu to Improve Employee Experience and Benefit Communications

April 20, 2021 | Pleasanton, Ca. – Truly Human Inc. (TruHu), a leading Employee Success SaaS company, today announces a national partnership to help EPIC Insurance Brokers & Consultants solve one of their diverse client base's most timely and universal problems: staying connected to their remote and mobile employees.

According to Craig Hasday, President of the National Employee Benefits Practice at EPIC, “The Pandemic has created lasting changes to the workplace. Many of our small- and medium-sized clients now face the unprecedented challenge of managing a remote and socially distanced workforce. Organizations must focus on building culture and remaining connected to maintain productivity and reduce turnover. Tools like TruHu Pulse help them engage and communicate with their employees, wherever they are. Not only are we helping solve our clients' problems but focusing on Employee Experience is a differentiator for EPIC."

Says founder Pete Yozzo, “We are excited to help EPIC's clients confront these new, post-pandemic realities. HR professionals need to improve employee communications and understand how they are thinking and feeling. With TruHu Pulse, it is easy for HR to send messages and surveys as well as share company benefit and HR information directly to employees’ phones."

About EPIC Insurance Brokers & Consultants

EPIC is a unique and innovative retail risk management and employee benefits insurance brokerage and consulting firm, founded in San Francisco, California in 2007 with offices and leadership across the country. EPIC has created a values-based, client-focused culture that attracts and retains top talent, fosters employee satisfaction and loyalty and sustains a high level of customer service excellence. EPIC possesses a depth of industry expertise across key lines of insurance, including risk management, property and casualty, employee benefits, unique specialty program insurance and private client services.
