My HR Portal

A simple, customizable way to centralize and share important HR, benefits, and enterprise information. Provide employees quick access to materials like handbooks, benefits/company policies, 401k links, open enrollment, and learning management systems. Customize My HR Portal with your company’s branding and use it to deliver personalized employee information.

Pulse Surveys

Now more than ever, companies need to monitor employee well-being. Enhance your organization’s ability to report employee engagement, improve company culture, and boost employee performance. Leverage TruHu’s library of almost 200 HR approved questions on Productivity, Diversity, Satisfaction, Benefits, COVID-19 and more.

My Benefits Portal

A tool developed for brokers to help ensure your employees get the most from their benefits. My Benefits Portal provides employees with one-click access to open enrollment documents, forms, and links to transactional systems. Send recurring surveys and open enrollment, benefits, or health-related messages.

Dynamic Messaging

Assure important communications reach an increasingly mobile workforce. With our trackable texts and emails, know a message’s open rate, and easily resend to those who did not view it. Customize your messages with links, images, attachments, HTML, and multilingual options. Segment them by individuals, departments, or office locations.
