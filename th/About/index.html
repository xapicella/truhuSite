A Human Story

Throughout my career I’ve felt intimately aware of and connected to the problems inherent to Small companies; especially those associated with employee happiness and productivity.

Yet as the CEO and founder of an HR Tech company that served over 100,000 employers, I faced a real HR problem of my own. We consistently lost top performers to large companies that were, frankly, doing a better job of nurturing their employees and engaging them in the company culture.

Let me say that again, we were an HR company and our employees were leaving...to work for larger companies. Not always for higher pay, but for a better employee experience.

After stepping down from an operating role in our company I had a chance to dig into the problem. The more I explored, the more intrigued I became. At its core, employee experience is the difference between success and failure. Large companies such as Salesforce, Workday, Google invest and work hard to engage their employees. As a result, their turnover is much lower, their cultures are sought after and their employees are more productive than almost all smaller companies. They’ve done the math and have figured out that it’s good business to invest in employee experience.

The inspiration for Truly Human Inc. was born.

The time has come to level the playing field and provide the same tools and return on investment to the small- to medium-sized companies that larger companies have been enjoying for a long time.

That’s why I joined forces with Cathy Reilly, an accomplished career HR executive and HR tech founder. Together we founded Truly Human Inc. (TruHu), to create amazing employee experience for the rest of us.

Pete Yozzo, CEO

Who We Are

TruHu (Truly Human, Inc.) was started in 2019 with a simple but powerful goal: help make an amazing employee experience possible at every company, regardless of size or industry. We at TruHu know that companies that deliver an exceptional employee experience enjoy greater productivity, reduced turnover and a stronger culture.

Developed by former benefits brokers and successful HR Tech entrepreneurs—including the founder of both Blaise Software (now Ebix) and ThinkHR—TruHu provides Brokers and Employers with simple tools that help them onboard, manage, and analyze their workforce.

TruHu's first product is Pulse, which provides employers with a mobile-first way to keep employees connected, engaged, and productive, no matter where they are.
